Atmasamyama Yoga

Path of Meditation

In this profound chapter, Krishna reveals the ancient science of meditation and self-control. He explains the characteristics of a true yogi, the process of meditation, and how to achieve perfect equanimity of mind. This chapter provides practical guidance for controlling the restless mind and attaining the highest state of consciousness through disciplined practice.

üßò Meditation Science üìø 47 Verses ‚öñÔ∏è Mental Control

In this transformative chapter on Dhyana Yoga, Krishna unveils the ancient science of meditation and mental discipline. He explains that true renunciation and yoga are one and the same, revealing the progressive stages of spiritual development. This chapter provides practical guidance for controlling the restless mind, achieving perfect mental equilibrium, and attaining the highest state of self-realization through systematic meditation practice.

‚ú® The Science of Self-Mastery

Krishna reveals that the mind can be either our greatest friend or our worst enemy. Through disciplined practice and gradual detachment from sense objects, one can elevate the consciousness and achieve perfect yoga. The chapter emphasizes that meditation is not about suppressing the mind, but about training it to become a tool for spiritual realization rather than material bondage.
