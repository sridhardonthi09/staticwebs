Jnana Yoga

Path of Knowledge

In this profound chapter, Krishna reveals the divine science of transcendental knowledge and the supreme secret of His eternal nature. He explains the purpose of divine incarnations, how to attain true wisdom, and the transformative power of spiritual knowledge. This chapter unveils the path of Jnana Yoga - liberation through divine knowledge and understanding.

Krishna reveals His eternal, unborn nature and explains the fundamental difference between divine consciousness and individual soul consciousness. This supreme knowledge forms the foundation of Jnana Yoga - the path of liberation through divine wisdom and understanding of one's true nature.
