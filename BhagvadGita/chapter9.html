Raja Vidya Raja Guhya Yoga

The Most Confidential Knowledge

This chapter reveals the royal knowledge and royal secret - the most confidential and supreme knowledge about Krishna's divine nature. It explains how everything emanates from Him, how He maintains the universe through His divine energy, and the supreme secret of devotional service that leads to liberation.

In this profound chapter on Raja Vidya Yoga, Krishna unveils the most confidential and royal knowledge - the supreme secret that reveals His transcendental nature and relationship with creation. This chapter is the pinnacle of spiritual wisdom, accessible only to those who approach with pure faith and devotion.

‚ú® The Divine Mystery

Krishna reveals the great paradox: while He pervades the entire universe and all beings exist in Him, He simultaneously remains completely detached from the material creation. This demonstrates His supreme yogic power (yoga-maya) and transcendental nature. He is the source and sustainer of everything, yet remains unaffected by material qualities.
