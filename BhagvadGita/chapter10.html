Vibhuti Vistara Yoga

The Opulence of the Absolute

In this magnificent chapter, Krishna reveals His divine manifestations and infinite opulences throughout creation. He describes how He pervades all existence as the source of power, beauty, strength, and knowledge in all beings and phenomena. This chapter inspires devotion by showing how the Divine can be perceived and worshipped through His countless manifestations in the universe.

üåü Divine Glory üìø 42 Verses ‚ú® Infinite Opulence

In this magnificent chapter on Vibhuti Vistara Yoga, Krishna reveals His divine manifestations and infinite opulences throughout creation. He explains that all extraordinary qualities, powers, beauty, and knowledge in the universe are but fragments of His splendor. This chapter provides a practical method for devotional service by teaching us to see and worship Krishna through His countless manifestations in all beings and phenomena.

‚ú® Seeing the Divine Everywhere

Krishna teaches that wherever we find exceptional beauty, power, knowledge, or any extraordinary quality, we should understand it as a manifestation of His divine energy. This vision transforms our relationship with the world from mundane to sacred, making every moment an opportunity for devotional remembrance and worship of the Supreme through His infinite manifestations.
